- Some 2025 physical pages are secure because the search string matched on that extracted page and the printed page number was visible in the same extraction. The 2025 preface footnote on physical p. 7 is secure as a physical page, but its printed-page label is less clean because it appears in the notes section after the preface text.
- Physical page numbers for the chapter 2 and chapter 8 abstracta passages were checked by targeted `pdftotext` searches, but I did not manually inspect rendered PDF pages.
- The negative 2020 SFL check is based on local Markdown search terms listed above. It corroborates the 2025 statement but is not itself a positive passage from *Norms and Necessity*.
